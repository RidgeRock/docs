## What is the Mutable Web?

The Mutable Web redefines how we interact with online content, empowering users and developers to dynamically modify and extend web pages without altering their core structure. 

Imagine seamlessly integrating tools, enhancing layouts, or deploying new features directly on live websites—all without breaking their original functionality.

**Key Features:**

- **Adaptability:** Tailor digital spaces to meet individual or organizational needs.
- **Interoperability:** Integrate third-party tools, widgets, and APIs effortlessly.
- **Non-Intrusive Design:** Ensure safety and compatibility without altering backend code.
- **User-Centric Innovation:** Empower users to shape their digital experiences collaboratively.

The Mutable Web creates a flexible, user-driven digital ecosystem that prioritizes control, creativity, and collaboration.
