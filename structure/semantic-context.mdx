## Why Semantic Contexts Matter

Imagine a world where every website adapts to your unique needs. Whether you seek enhanced accessibility, streamlined workflows, or advanced tools, **Semantic Contexts** make it possible. This isn’t just a convenience—it’s a paradigm shift in how we interact with the internet.

* **Empowerment:** Take charge of your browsing experience with customizations that matter to you.

* **Inclusivity:** Ensure web content is accessible to everyone, regardless of abilities or circumstances.

### Breaking Free from Limitations

Traditional websites are rigid, with developers defining how platforms look and function. While standardization offers consistency, it often alienates users with specific needs. **Semantic Contexts** break these limitations by enabling users to:

* Overlay new functionalities.

* Modify appearances.

* Enhance usability—all without altering the original website’s code.

This balance between flexibility and security ensures a seamless experience for everyone.

## How It Works

Semantic Contexts operate through a **modular** and **decentralized** framework. Here’s how:

### Mutations: The Building Blocks of Change

**What Are Mutations?**
Mutations are modular units of functionality—ranging from small tweaks to major overhauls—that transform how a website behaves or looks. Whether it’s adding **dark mode**, **real-time data widgets**, or **enhanced UI elements**, mutations provide unmatched flexibility.

**How Do Mutations Work?**
Mutations act as overlays, modifying content without touching the underlying website code. This guarantees both **compatibility** and **security** while enabling dynamic customization.
