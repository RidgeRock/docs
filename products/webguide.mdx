## What is WebGuide?

WebGuide overlays helpful tools onto websites, making them more interactive and personalized. Key features include:

* **Contextual Navigation**: Highlights relevant links and shortcuts.

* **Content Summaries**: Quickly understand articles or web pages.

* **Accessibility Tools**: Includes dark mode, text resizing, and more.
